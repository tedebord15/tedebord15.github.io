Data Encoding Table

Riding a bike
Component Type Number of bits/range Description
Weather ASCII 256 bits (0-32 characters) A description of the weather outside when you go on the bike ride.
Temperature Number 7 bits (0-127 degrees) The temperature when you go on the bike ride.
How long it took Number 1-8 bits (0-255 minutes) The amount of time to finish the bike ride in minutes.
Where you went ASCII 256 bits (0-32 characters) Where you went on the bike ride.
Who you went with ASCII 512 bits (0-64 characters) Who you went with on the bike ride.
